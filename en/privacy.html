Privacy Policy

Version date: 2026-04-22

This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed when using the website https://cv.bazlove.com/en/, for what purposes such data is used, and how a user can contact the operator regarding privacy matters.

1. Operator

Operator: Website owner.

Privacy contact: Email Address.

2. What data may be processed

  • Technical and analytics-related data about visits to the website, including IP address, browser and device information, interface language, date and time of visit, page URL, referrer, and technical on-site events.
  • Data processed through Yandex.Metrica, including cookies, localStorage identifiers, and analytics-related behavioral data.
  • Data about interaction with website pages through analytics and Session Replay / Webvisor, including page views, scrolling, clicks, and other user interactions, to the extent made available by the analytics service.
  • Data voluntarily provided by the user when contacting the operator via email, Telegram, LinkedIn, or other communication channels.

3. Purposes of processing

  • to maintain website functionality and security;
  • to obtain aggregated website traffic and behavior statistics;
  • to analyze usability and improve the structure and quality of content;
  • to process inbound user inquiries;
  • to respond to hiring, collaboration, consulting, advisory, or project-related requests.

4. Legal grounds for processing

Data may be processed on the following grounds:

  • user consent, where such consent is required by applicable law;
  • the operator’s legitimate interests in maintaining, securing, and improving the website;
  • the need to process and respond to a user’s request where the user has initiated contact.

5. Third-party services and providers

The website may use Yandex.Metrica for web analytics, including Session Replay / Webvisor.

The website is hosted on GitHub Pages and related technical infrastructure used for hosting and content delivery.

The operator does not sell personal data to third parties.

6. Cookies and local storage

The website may use cookies and localStorage for analytics, technical preferences, and service stability.

The user can restrict or disable cookies in browser settings. Doing so may affect certain website functions and the accuracy of analytics.

7. Data retention

Data is retained no longer than necessary for the purposes described above, or for the periods defined by analytics service settings and applicable legal requirements.

User inquiries and business correspondence may be retained for a reasonable period necessary to process the request, continue communication, and protect the operator’s legitimate interests.

8. Cross-border transfer

Because the website relies on external hosting, CDN, analytics, and technical service providers, certain technical data processing may involve cross-border transfer or access from other jurisdictions.

The operator aims to use only the services and data volume necessary for website operation and basic analytics.

9. User rights

Where applicable under the law, the user may:

  • contact the operator with questions related to data processing on the website;
  • request information about the nature of data processing within the information and technical capabilities available to the operator;
  • withdraw consent where processing is based on consent and such consent is required by applicable law;
  • independently restrict analytics data collection through browser settings, cookie restrictions, and Yandex.Metrica opt-out tools, where such tools are available for the user’s browser or device.

The operator reviews requests in good faith, but cannot guarantee fulfillment of requests regarding data processed and stored by third-party analytics services outside the operator’s direct technical control.
